This site is an interactive map of the global events and boss locations for the VRMMO Zenith: The Last City.

  • Right-click on the map and selecting "Add a waypoint here" to place a marker to record location.
  • Right-click on the map and select "Copy coordinate URL" to copy the url.
    When this url is accessed, the map will open with a white marker placed at the clicked coordinates.
    You can share the url and tell other players about the meeting place, etc.
  • You can select the type of marker to be displayed by clicking "Toggle view" in the lower left corner.

右上のドロップダウンから言語を変更できます。
You can change the language from the drop-down in the upper right corner.
